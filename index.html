We Are Hip-Hop

Katman and Noobs are Jerusalem-based Jazz musicians, trained at the Jerusalem Academy of Music and Dance. After finishing their studies and army service, the duo went on to make small productions, notably with local rapper GushDan, with whom they put out the successful album - Kaki Naknik. Now they are open to expand into further collaborations and musical explorations and waiting to find the next partner to work with. Are you ready?
